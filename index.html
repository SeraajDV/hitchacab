Our mission

We provide all Capetonians a carpooling platform where we connect drivers with passengers.

Our goal is to make transport in the city safer, more reliable and accessible to everyone and providing a form of income to all hitchacab drivers.

Features

Join a group with people in your area

Request a lift or offer a lift

The app can be implemented by groups of people aswell, in the case of a group of friends planning on going to the same destination then they can offer one another a lift instead of everyone going on there own to the same place.

Use the group chat to plan your route.
